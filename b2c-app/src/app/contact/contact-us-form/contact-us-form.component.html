Share Your Feedback

We love feedback - good and bad! If you want to pay us a compliment or you have an issue with our service, please let us know. We will do our best to try and sort it out for you. We aim to resolve complaints quickly and fairly with all our customers and affiliates.
To submit your feedback:
  • Email feedback@travelstart.com.ng with the Travelstart booking reference number in the subject line.
  • In the message section, please write a detailed explanation of the feedback or complaint. The more detailed information you provide the easier it is for us to solve your problem quickly
  • A member of the dispute resolution team will get back to you between Mondays and Fridays - 9am to 5pm
