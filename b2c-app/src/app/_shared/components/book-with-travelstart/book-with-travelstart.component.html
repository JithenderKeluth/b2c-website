How to book flights with us

1. Enter your departure city, arrival destination, and travel dates and search.
2. Select the flight option most suited to your schedule and budget.
3. Insert the correct passenger details according to passport / ID book.
4. Opt to purchase rebookable flights, baggage or travel insurance.
5. Select your preferred payment method.
choose payments
6. Once payment is received, you will receive booking confirmation via email.
7. Passengers have to be at the airport at least one hour before domestic departures and at least three hours before international departures.
8. New Immigration Regulation dictates that as from 01 June 2015, children under 18 require Unabridged Birth Certificates, or Birth Certificates Containing Parental Details. This requirement applies in addition to the requirement of a passports when travelling internationally. Should you be travelling with/making a reservation for a child, please ensure that you apply for these certificates timeously.
9. Please ensure that you check the visa and inoculation requirements for all transit points.
10. Passengers travelling internationally must ensure that their passports are valid for 6 months after your return to South Africa. Please also ensure that you have a minimum of two blank pages in your passport.
